  • ONE Use case: DWELL TIME PREDICTIoN TO FIGHT UNPRODUCTIVE MOVES

    The container dwell time is predicted by our algorithms to cut unproductive moves, their costs and better allocate terminal resources?

    Dwell Time Prediction

    Terminal operators stack containers in the yard area in piles that can be up to 7 containers high. Only inbound and outbound moves are productive moves. Moves from yard to yard are considered unproductive as they bring no value to the terminal. The reason is that each time a truck arrives for a container pick-up, all containers stacked on top of that one are reshuffled to move it onto the truck. These moves are costly in terms of committed resources, as a large amount of machines and man-hours are mobilized for these unproductive moves. Also, it weakens quality of service to the customer and hinders the terminal new contracts.. The actual loss and opportunity cost are considerable for terminal operators.

    Our goal is to avoid unproductive moves by knowing how long a container will stay in the yard in comparison to the other containers. By knowing the dwell time, you can store the containers accordingly, and there will be no more unproductive moves.
